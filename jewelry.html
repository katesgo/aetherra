Caring for Your Copper

Patina

Copper naturally darkens and develops a rich patina over time. This is the beauty of the metal — embrace the transformation.

Cleaning

Mix equal parts vinegar and salt to restore shine. Polish with a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals.

Water

Remove before swimming, bathing, or washing hands to prolong the finish. Copper reacts with moisture over time.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place — ideally in a pouch or lined jewelry box to prevent scratches.
